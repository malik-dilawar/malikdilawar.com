About Me

I am Malik Dilawar, a seasoned professional in the outsourcing and BPO industry, with a robust background in insurance and business administration. My journey has spanned over five years in the insurance sector, where I honed my skills in business development, team leadership, and strategic planning.

As a Co-founder and Chief Operating Officer (COO) of DH Malik Global, I oversee the operational aspects of our business, ensuring that our processes align with our company’s vision. My role allows me to drive operational excellence and foster innovation within our regional markets, building strong relationships along the way.

With a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from the International Islamic University, Islamabad, and a Google-certified specialization in Digital Marketing & E-commerce, I bring a blend of academic knowledge and practical expertise to the table. I am passionate about leveraging my experience to explore new opportunities and achieve shared goals with like-minded professionals.
